## How It Works

1. **Share Your Link** - Send your referral link to friends and family
2. **They Switch** - Your friends sign up to Octopus Energy using your link
3. **You Both Win** - You both receive £50 bill credit when they become active customers

## Terms & Conditions

- New customers only (must not have been with Octopus Energy in the last 12 months)
- £50 credit applied once the new customer becomes an active customer
- Credit is applied to bill account within 30 days
- Limited to one referral bonus per new customer
